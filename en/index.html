About me

I am a software developer with 7 years of experience in both back-end and front-end, working in different industries (Binova, Softtech, Innova and CK Boğaziçi and ATP).

During the time I worked, I took part in projects within the scope of web and windows application solutions for customer demands, windows application development for credit card modules, web application development for retail banking activities, and virtual branches of financial institutions. The majority of these projects have been developed with C # ASP.NET MVC. In addition, I have made improvements using AngularJS and ReactJS for the projects written from scratch.

As a person who is open to learning new things at any time, I can quickly integrate into different platforms, applications in different fields and projects. I see being ambitious and self-sacrificing in my work as my prominent aspect.

I am actively working as a Senior Software Development Specialist at ATP I am a graduate of Istanbul Technical University Computer Engineering. You can get in touch with me from this link.
